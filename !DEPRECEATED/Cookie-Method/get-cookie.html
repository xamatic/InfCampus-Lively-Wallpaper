🍪 Get Your Session Cookie

⚠️ Important: You must be logged into the school portal first!

Step 1: Log In

Open this link in a new tab and log in:

https://410.ncsis.gov/campus/resources/portal/grades

Step 2: Extract Cookie Using JavaScript

After logging in, come back to this page and click this button:

Click the button to extract your cookie...

Step 3: Manual Extraction (If automatic fails)

  1. While on the grades page, press F12 to open Developer Tools
  2. Go to the Application tab (Chrome) or Storage tab (Firefox)
  3. Click on Cookies in the left sidebar
  4. Click on https://410.ncsis.gov
  5. Find the cookie named JSESSIONID
  6. Copy the entire Value (usually a long string of random characters)

Step 4: Use the Cookie

Copy the cookie value and paste it into your widget's "Setup Authentication" section.

Format: Just paste the value (without "JSESSIONID=")

✅ Cookie extracted! Copy it from the box above and paste it into your widget.
